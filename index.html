What We Do

Our
Services

01
Exterior Detail
Hand wash, clay bar treatment, paint decontamination, and a professional wax or sealant to restore deep gloss and protect your paint.
02
Interior Clean
Deep vacuum, steam cleaning, leather conditioning, dashboard and trim detailing, odor elimination, and window cleaning.
03
Wheel & Tyre
Iron fallout removal, wheel decontamination, tyre dressing, and brake dust cleaning for a complete wheel bay transformation.
04
Paint Correction
Multi-stage machine polishing to remove swirl marks, scratches, oxidation and water spots. Restore mirror-like clarity and depth.
05
Ceramic Coating
Nano-ceramic protection that bonds to your paint, creating a hydrophobic, scratch-resistant shield lasting years — not weeks.
06
Headlight Restore
Sand, polish, and UV seal cloudy, yellowed headlights back to crystal clarity — dramatically improving looks and road safety.
